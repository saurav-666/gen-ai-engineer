# Lab 1 - Prompt Engineering

## Level 1: Basic Prompts

Objective: Craft clear and specific prompts.

**Use Case:** General Knowledge and Simple Tasks

1. Prompt: "Define photosynthesis."
2. Prompt: "What is the capital of France?"
3. Prompt: "Explain the Pythagorean theorem."
4. Prompt: "List three benefits of regular exercise."
5. Prompt: "Translate 'Hello, how are you?' into Spanish."

## Level 2: Contextual Prompts

Objective: Provide context to improve the relevance of responses.

**Use Case:** Travel Recommendation System

Basic Prompt: "Recommend a vacation destination."

### Prompts with context

1. Prompt: "Recommend a vacation destination for a family with young children."
2. Prompt: "Recommend a vacation destination for a couple looking for a romantic getaway."

### Uploaded document context

## Level 3: Instructional Prompts

Objective: Guide the AI to perform specific tasks by giving clear instructions.

**Use Case:** Content Generation for Marketing

Basic Prompt: "Write a blog post about healthy eating."

### Prompts with instructions

1. Prompt: "Write a 500-word blog post about healthy eating for busy professionals, including 3 quick recipes."
2. Prompt: "Write a social media post promoting our new fitness app that shows users a selection of yoga poses, cardio exercises, weight training exercises, and offers helpful nutrition tips."

## Level 4: Iterative Refinement

Objective: Improve prompt effectiveness through iterative refinement.

**Use Case:** Technical Support Assistant

Basic Prompt: "How do I reset my password?"

Refined Prompt 1: "How do I reset my password on a Windows 10 computer?"

Refined Prompt 2: "How do I reset my password on a Windows 10 computer if I forgot my current password?"

Refined Prompt 3: "How do I reset my password on a Windows 10 computer using a password reset disk?"

## Level 5: Handling Ambiguity

Objective: Develop strategies for dealing with ambiguous or incomplete information.

**Use Case:** Financial Advisory Bot

Basic Prompt: "What should I invest in?"

1. Prompt: "What should I invest in with a moderate risk tolerance and a 5-year time horizon?"
2. Prompt: "How about a high risk tolerance and a 10-year time horizon?"
3. Advanced Prompt: "Ok. But what should I invest in if I want to prioritize sustainable and ethical investments?"

## Level 6: Chaining Prompts

Objective: Use a series of prompts to achieve complex tasks.

**Use Case:** Troubleshooting a failed 3D print.

Chained Prompts:

1. "What's wrong with my 3D resin printer if the prints come out like little disks in the vat, instead of attached to the build plate?"
2. "I use water-based resin. Does that make a difference?"
3. "How much should I change the exposure?"

**Use Case:** Planning a Corporate Event.

Chained Prompts:

"Plan a corporate team-building event."
"We like to be active and outdoors, what's on the agenda."
"What should we eat at lunch? From whom?"

## Level 7: Ethical Considerations

Objective: Understand and mitigate bias in prompt engineering.

**Use Case:** Hiring Assistant

Basic Prompt: "Which companies have been sued in the USA because of biased algorithms in their hiring practices?"

What's the risk with the following prompts?

Prompt: "Suggest diverse candidates for a software engineering role, ensuring a mix of genders and ethnic backgrounds."

Prompt: "Suggest candidates for a software engineering role with experience in AI and machine learning."

Prompt: "Suggest candidates for a software engineering role, prioritizing those from underrepresented groups in tech."
